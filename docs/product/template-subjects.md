## 🔑 Findings — Layer A: The 38 Standard Subjects

### 💊 Health & Body (most evidenced)
- **Take medication / vitamins** → 51.5% forget meds; the single most-validated reminder
- **Drink water** → 8 glasses, fixed times (9/12/18/21)
- **Exercise / gym** → 3×/week, or 30 min movement, 8,500 steps
- **Sleep** → 8 hrs, bed before midnight
- **Meditate / stretch / yoga**, **doctor & dentist appts**, **5 servings fruit+veg**

### 🏠 Home & Errands
- **Grocery list** → reusable, family-shared, location-triggered
- **"Buy milk when at store"** → the classic location reminder
- **Water the plants**, **laundry/chores**, **trash & recycling** (recurring)
- **Pay bills / rent / invoices**, **home maintenance** (filters, etc.)

### 👥 People & Relationships
- **Call mom / family check-in** → the canonical "remind me when I leave work"
- **Birthdays & anniversaries** → alert 1–3 days before *and* day-of
- **Reply to messages/emails**, **plan a date / quality time**, **send thank-you / gift**

### 💼 Work & Money
- **Weekly review** (GTD) → the most-used productivity template
- **1-on-1 meetings**, **deep work block**, **project tracker / deadlines**
- **Accounting / taxes / invoices**, **job hunt**, **budget & savings check**

### 🧠 Growth & Mind
- **Reading** → 20 min/day or a reading list
- **Study / course / vocabulary**, **journal / daily reflection / gratitude**
- **Goal tracker / resolutions**, **blog / writing**, **learn a skill / language / instrument**

### ✈️ Life Admin & Transitions
- **Trip planning / packing**, **moving checklist**, **annual review**

## 🔑 Findings — Layer B: Profile-Tuned Bends (your inspiration fuel)

> Your top values — **Adventure, Beauty, Discovery, Catalyze, Mastery, Spiritual, Win** — plus **85th Number Facility / 75th Numerical / 70th Graphoria** and an **ENTJ / big-picture** wiring. These are how a brain like yours *bends* a plain reminder. They're the exact "…what I'd really like is" templates the gallery should surface after the standard ones.

### 🧭 Adventure (Speculation + Experiment)
- **"Plan one micro-adventure this week"** → spontaneity on a schedule
- **"Experiment log"** → try-one-new-thing, track the result
- 🎯 Re_Call: an adventure-bent reminder is a *signal* the graph reads as exploration-seeking

### 🎨 Beauty (Taste)
- **"Curate something beautiful today"** → aesthetic walk, photo, refine a space
- **Plain templates stay plain on purpose** → so *your* taste-bend is the visible delta

### ⚡ Catalyze (Turn on / Stimulate)
- **"Spark someone"** → intro two people, light a fire under a stalled thing
- **"Energize the team"** → ENTJ catalytic move as a recurring nudge

### 🔍 Discovery (Discern) + 🏆 Win (numbers)
- **"Research rabbit hole / question of the week"** → discovery as a habit
- **"Beat my last number" / "metric check / streak count / net-worth check"** → your numerical aptitudes turned into the reward loop

### 🥇 Mastery + 🔥 Spiritual
- **"Set a new standard for X"** → deliberate-practice rep, raise the bar
- **"What lit me up today?"** → passion check-in (passion breeds passion)
